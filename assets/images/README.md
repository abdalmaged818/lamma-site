# Image folders

- `logo/`: the official Lamma logo. Replace `lamma-logo.svg` only.
- `gatherings/`: approved gathering photos.
- `fellowship/`: approved Creative Fellowship photos.
- `spaces/`: approved Shared Spaces photos.
- `community/`: approved community photos and stories.
- `general/`: shared or general-purpose imagery.

The `placeholder.svg` files are temporary and may be replaced when final imagery is available.
